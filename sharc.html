WOWs Ship shell arcs

Sharc is a comparison tool for different ships shell arcs and fly times, based on World of Warships data.

General info

AFT (based on caliber) and spotting aircraft (every ship) automatically included. Default properties in place of internal data (shell drag / krupp values).
